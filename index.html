Mistaking time with destiny's glance,
I stumble through my existence,
Faces of the past now lost in the mists,
No longer comprehending the distance.

So much has changed since I reached maturity,
Yet still, all seems like a dream,
The information swirling in my mind,
Leaving me lost and unsure, it seems.

Through loss and gain, I still remain,
A static figure in a world of change,
As silence breaks and tsunamis pass,
I grow and evolve, but still, I feel the same.

The faces of the past now fade away,
As I move forward on my path,
But their memory lingers, haunting my days,
Reminding me of the past I cannot grasp.

Time and destiny,
Their paths, forever intertwined,
But I will forge my own way,
And leave the past behind.
